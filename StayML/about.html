About

Background

Founded in 2020 in Norway, as part of the Antler Global, Stay ML has years of experience in financial analytics, spatial analytics and investment banking covering tech and commercial real estate.

Focus

We address the need of Property Owners, Real estate investors, Brokers and their stakeholders to predict tenant behavior. This is done by calculating the risk of churn using machine learning.

Strategy

We aim to be the market leader and de facto standard for churn prediction and risk assessment in the real estate industry. We are an innovative, privately owned specialist Cloud ML Solution Vendor.

Solution

Our main solution is Churn Risk Assessment (CRA), for the risk management and valuation analysis of commercial real estate portfolio.

Future

This state-of-the art software solution has an extremely flexible data model that enables input of virtually any existing or future datatype.

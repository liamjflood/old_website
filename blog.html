Design Thinking 10 Nov 2020

The main benefit of design thinking is that it is fully focussed on the end user. It provides a structured way to get fast feedback and ensures that development time is not wasted on ideas that the end user does not consider valuable.

One of the best resources for this is the Design Thinking Bootleg Deck from Stanford d.school.
